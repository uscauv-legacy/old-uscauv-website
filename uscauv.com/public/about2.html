About Us

Since 2003, the University of Southern California Autonomous Underwater Vehicle Team (USC AUV) has consistently participated in the annual International Autonomous Underwater Vehicle (AUV) competition of the Association for Unmanned Vehicle Systems International (AUVSI). The competition, hosted by AUVSI and the Office of Naval Research (ONR), challenges top universities from around the world to design, build, and program autonomous underwater vehicles capable of completing a series of tasks. These tasks represent real applications of underwater robotics currently encountered in industry and academia.

USC AUV is a student-run organization that works to advance underwater robotics research and methods at USC. We are committed to researching, designing, and building a completely autonomous underwater vehicle (AUV).
