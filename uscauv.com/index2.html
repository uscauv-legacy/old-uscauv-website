About

USC Autonomous Underwater Vehicle Team (USC AUV) represents the Viterbi School of Engineering to the international engineering community through innovation, leadership, and excellence in the field of unmanned systems.

The student-led team focuses on the design and creation of an autonomous underwater vehicle for the annual international RoboSub competition, a challenging and competitive event that is co-hosted by the U.S. Office of Naval Research and the Association for Unmanned Vehicle Systems International.

Greater LA Botball Tournament

The annual regional tournament allows the students to showcase their hard work and compete with other teams from all over the Greater LA region. Participants' robots are programmed to maneuver on a custom playing field in order to gain points for achieving certain tasks.
